Smart Blind Stick(IOT)

Developed a cutting-edge Smart Blind Stick IoT project, a revolutionary assistive technology solution aimed at enhancing the mobility and safety of visually impaired individuals. By promoting independence and safety, this project represents a significant advancement in assistive technology for the visually impaired.
